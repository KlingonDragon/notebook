Starfleet 2409|Xindi-Arboreal (Alien)|Engineering|Universe-Class|Δ

Transported from the mid-22nd to the late-24th century as a child by the sphere-builders as part of a plan to infiltrate Starfleet, contact soon ceased and she was subsequently assimilated; she was quickly rescued by a nearby Starfleet ship. Having then grown up in the modern federation, her allegience has since shifted and she joined Starfleet Academy.
